# QOL Foundation — Base Statutes (Draft)

## Name

QOL Foundation (QOLChain Foundation)

## Purpose

To steward the QOLChain standard, manage resources for its maintenance, promote adoption, and resolve disputes requiring institutional intervention.

## Activities

* Maintain the official repository and whitepaper.
* Provide auditing and verification services for third parties.
* Coordinate arbitration panels.
* Raise funds for development and creator subsidies.

## Governance

* Founding Council (3–7 members): responsible for vision and compliance.
* Technical Council (developers): maintains the protocol and manages merges.
* Ethics Committee: reviews rules and sensitive disputes.

## Funding

* Donations, moderate opt-in transaction fees, grants, and sponsorships.
* Annual audited financial transparency.

## Jurisdiction

* Recommended: register in a jurisdiction with a favorable framework for technology NGOs (e.g., Switzerland, Estonia, U.S. 501(c)(3), depending on strategy).

*This is a draft. Local legal counsel is recommended for formal incorporation.*
